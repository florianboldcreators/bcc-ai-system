## Workflow Steps

### 1. Briefing (PM ‚Üí Creative Lead)
- Comes via Asana task with brand, product, timeline
- Often includes reference links (TikTok trends, competitor content)
- May include client-specific requirements or restrictions
- Budget info if production is involved

### 2. Concept Development (Creative Lead)
- Research current trends on TikTok/Instagram
- Develop concept with hook, storyline, visual direction
- Write production brief with specific directions for editor
- Create mockups/visual references if needed (AI image generation)
- Document in Asana task notes

### 3. Internal Review (PM reviews concept)
- Hallhuber reviews for brand fit and feasibility
- Feedback loop until approved
- Production budget check

### 4. Client Approval (PM ‚Üí Client)
- PM presents to client (not Creative Lead directly)
- Client feedback incorporated

### 5. Production (Producer + Editor)
- Mert coordinates shoots/production
- Jose/Malorie edit based on creative brief
- Creative Lead reviews cuts for alignment with concept

### 6. Publishing & Reporting
- Community Manager handles posting
- Performance tracked and fed back into next concepts
