# Step 3 — Caption Refinement (Detailed)

## Purpose
Take approved concept(s) from Hopper review and produce final, publish-ready captions for Instagram and TikTok.

## Prerequisites
- Concept(s) approved by Hopper (passed Review Scorecard with avg ≥ 7/10)
- If Hopper requested revisions, those must be incorporated first

## Instagram Caption Rules

### Reels (max 1,000 characters)
- Hook line first (question, bold statement, or emoji-led)
- 2-3 short paragraphs with line breaks
- CTA before hashtags (save, comment, share prompt)
- 8-12 hashtags at the end, separated by line break

### Carousels (max 1,500 characters)
- Can be slightly longer — more storytelling space
- Each slide's key point can be reinforced in caption
- Same CTA + hashtag rules as Reels

## TikTok Description Rules (max 300 characters)
- Punchy, one-liner or two-liner
- Leverage TikTok search keywords naturally
- 3-5 hashtags, no line break needed
- No long-form storytelling — the video IS the story

## Native German Quality Standards

### Syntax
- **V2 rule:** Finite verb in second position in main clauses
  - ✅ "Dieses Rezept verändert alles."
  - ❌ "Dieses Rezept alles verändert."
- **Verb-final in subclauses:** "...weil es so einfach ist."
- **No English word order leaking in:** Watch for SVO patterns that feel translated

### Idioms & Phrasing
- ❌ "Es ist ein Game-Changer" → ✅ "Das ändert alles"
- ❌ "Wir sind excited" → ✅ "Wir können es kaum erwarten"
- ❌ "Check das aus" → ✅ "Schau dir das an" (unless intentionally casual/Gen-Z)
- Exception: Some English loanwords are natural in German social media (Vibe, Aesthetic, Content). Use judgment.

### Rhythm
- Short sentences dominate: "Drei Zutaten. Fünf Minuten. Fertig."
- Avoid nested subclauses longer than 15 words
- Read it aloud — if you stumble, rewrite
