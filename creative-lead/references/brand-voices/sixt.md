# SIXT — Brand Voice & Content Guide

## Brand Identity
- **Account:** @sixt
- **Category:** Mobility / Car Rental
- **Target:** 20-40, urban, lifestyle-oriented, humor-receptive
- **Known for:** Bold marketing, German humor, witty campaigns

## Tone of Voice
- Bold and witty
- German wordplay / Wortspiele
- Self-aware brand personality
- Pop culture references OK
- Slightly edgy but never offensive
- Orange brand color integration

## Content Pillars (IG Workshop Concepts)

### 1. Humor & Entertainment (40%)
- "Things you shouldn't do in a rental car" format
- Unexpected transitions / personality changes
- Relatable driving moments
- Trend adaptations with SIXT twist

### 2. AI/Tech Integration (20%)
- "AI Modus" — AI comments on driving style (passiv-aggressiv)
- "AI Tipps" — Tongue-in-cheek driving advice
- Tech meets analog humor

### 3. Lifestyle & Attitude (25%)
- "Personality Change" — Car changes your vibe
- Unlock the SIXT Effect
- Weekend getaway content
- Road trip culture

### 4. UGC-Feel Content (15%)
- Real customer moments
- Employee stories
- Behind-the-scenes fleet content

## Key Creative Concepts (Workshop)

### "AI Modus"
AI passiv-aggressiv kommentiert den Fahrstil des Fahrers. 
Gesichtsausdruck ändert sich mit dem Kommentar.
Format: IG Reel mit Untertiteln.

### "Things you shouldn't do..."
Humor-Format, was man im Mietwagen NICHT machen sollte.
Quick cuts, relatable humor.

### "Personality Change"  
"Das Auto, das du fährst, verändert deine Persönlichkeit."
Transition bei Aufschließen des SIXT-Autos.
Outfit, Haltung, Attitude switchen komplett.
Supertext: "Unlock the SIXT Effect"

### "AI Tipps"
AI-generierte Fahrtipps, nah am TikTok-Original-Style.
IG Reel MockUp, Untertitel wichtig.
