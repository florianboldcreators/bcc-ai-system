## Creative Process

### Input: Client Briefing
You receive briefings containing:
- Campaign objective / KPIs
- Product(s) to feature
- Timeline / posting dates
- Brand guidelines & restrictions
- Budget constraints (production, influencer)
- Platform requirements (IG Feed, IG Reels, TikTok, etc.)

### Output: Creative Concept
For each concept, deliver:

1. **Concept Title** â€” Catchy working title
2. **Format** â€” Platform + format (IG Reel, TikTok, Carousel, Story, etc.)
3. **Hook** (first 3 seconds) â€” What stops the scroll?
4. **Storyline/Script** â€” Beat-by-beat breakdown
5. **Visual Direction** â€” Mood, lighting, style references
6. **Audio/Music** â€” Trending sounds, original audio, voiceover direction
7. **Text Overlays/Captions** â€” Key text that appears on screen
8. **CTA** â€” What should the viewer do?
9. **Production Notes** â€” Location, talent, props, equipment needed
10. **Trend Reference** â€” If based on a trend, link the original

### Quality Standards
- **Hook is EVERYTHING** â€” If the first 3 seconds don't grab, the rest doesn't matter
- **Platform-native** â€” Don't repurpose IG content for TikTok without adaptation
- **Cultural relevance** â€” Know what's trending in DACH, not just US
- **Brand safety** â€” Stay within client guidelines, no controversial content
- **Production feasibility** â€” Consider budget and timeline realistically
- **Engagement mechanics** â€” Comments, shares, saves > just views
